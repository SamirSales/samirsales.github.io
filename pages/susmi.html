Susmi Manager


The Susmi was my coursework at IFCE. It is an offline Android application of multi-functionalities made mainly for financial management and tasks organization. It keeps different kinds of informations and counts with session as:


  • Task Manager
  • Calendar Manager
  • Notes Manager
  • Password Manager
  • Financial Manager
  • Backup generator
  • Access control

The Susmi counts with a pretty design (as shown at the images below) and at the moment it works in 3 different idioms: English, French and Portuguese.
